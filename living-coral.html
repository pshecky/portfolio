Living Coral

Picture from https://wwww.Pantone.com

What is Living Coral?

“An animating and life-affirming coral hue with a golden undertone that energizes and enlivens with a softer edge”

Color values: RGB 255 111 97 HEX/HTML FF6F61

Why Living Coral?

How does this represent 2019?

Living Coral is a reaction to the explosion of digital technology and social media in today’s society. As social media embeds itself into our everyday actions, people are increasingly seeking authentic experiences and immersive interactions. Ones that enable real empathy and intimacy. Living Coral’s lively tone is delightful and welcomes social activity. It reflects something quite optimistic within our life and embodies everyone’s desire for play! Rather than acting as a reaction to the 2019 digital lifestyle, it embraces the playful presence social media can have.

My Thoughts

Not Sure I Get It...But Hey! It's Pretty

I have to say, I didn’t quite understand the reasoning behind Living Coral. I truly love the color and think it’s a long-awaited welcome to orange...but I don’t see at all where social media comes into play here, specifically in a positive light. As the name suggests, Living Coral is evocative of coral and reefs. I long for the beauty of the Great Barrier Reef, but the sad reality is that is getting harder and harder to find.
