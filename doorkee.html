Roles and Responsibility



Doorkee is an all-in-one apartment rental platform that connects Apartment Seekers, Departing Tenants, and Landlords. It streamlines the rental process by cutting out the broker, eliminating extra fees, and reducing building vacancies.



During my internship, I was responsible for conducting competitive research, creating a brand identity and voice, copywriting, and launching their social media presence. I also planned marketing events and designed marketing collateral during closed beta testing.

As their Social Media Manager, I curate and post content that aligns with Doorkee's brand voice and engage with users on these social platforms.
