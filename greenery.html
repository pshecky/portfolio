Greenery

What is Greenery?

“Greenery is a fresh and zesty yellow-green shade that evokes the first days of spring when nature’s greens revive, restore and renew.”

Color values: RGB 136 176 75 HEX/HTML 88B04B

Why Greenery?

How does this represent 2017?

Greenery is a “refreshing and revitalizing shade” which, as Pantone claims, is exactly what we need in 2017’s tumultuous political and social climate. This shade of green allows us to get back in touch with nature and our environment, signaling to consumers to take a deep breath and relax. This shade is also emblematic of the “pursuit of personal passions and vitality,” encouraging individual revitalization as well as societal.

My Thoughts

A Green Wake-Up Call

I think Greenery was an apt choice for 2017—while it didn’t quite reflect the political and social climate, it offered an alternative to it and a grounding force. I think this green shade is really powerful beyond the specific tensions of the year and the ongoing modernization and urbanization of our world. We are seeing less and less green and more and more grey. Less and less rainforests and gardens and more skyscrapers and highways. Greenery, to me, is a wake up call that if we continue on our current trajectory there will be no greenery left.
